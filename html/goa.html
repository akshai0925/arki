Anjuna Beach is a beach in Goa, which is located 18 kilometers
from Panaji and 8 kilometers to the west of Mapusa, North Goa. It is situated
in Anjuna village of Bardez taluka in North Goa. The beach is part of a 30
kilometer stretch of extended beach coastline along the west coast of Goa
by the Arabian Sea.
