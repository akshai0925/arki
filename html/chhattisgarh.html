barnawapara wildlifer

The Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary is located in Baloda Bazar
Bhatapara district. The reserve is about 100 km from Raipur and about 45 km
from Mahasamund city. It was established in 1976 under the Wildlife
Protection Act. The Jonk River flows through the sanctuary, which is a tributary
of the Mahanadi river.

at Rajim. A 7th century CE inscription recording the construction of the temple,
and dated to the reign of the king Vilasatunga, has been found here.

to Abhanpur; from there a left turn leads a narrow but motorable road to Rajim.
